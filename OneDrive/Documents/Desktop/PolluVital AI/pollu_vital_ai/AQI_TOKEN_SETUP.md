# How to get free AQI token and add it in PolluVital AI

1. Visit: https://aqicn.org/data-platform/token/
2. Create a free account and verify email.
3. Click **Create Token** and copy your token string.
4. Open PolluVital AI app.
5. Tap the **key icon** on top-right (**Set AQI Token**).
6. Paste token and press **Save**.
7. Start a scan. The app fetches Delhi AQI and caches it for offline usage.
